Welcome to South Central Zone Cultural Centre


An overloaded calendar of activities has kept the sprawling South Central Zone Cultural Centre buzzing with events over the last one year. With a makeover done in September last year, the Centre has been making news for all the right reasons in the cultural circles and has emerged as a favourite venue for all kinds of art, theatre and music related activities.
